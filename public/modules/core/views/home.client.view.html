About The Incremental Games Plaza

The Incremental Games Plaza is a community curated list of incremental games. This includes clicker games, idle games, unlocking games, etc.

What sets us apart from lists like the Ultimate List of Incremental Games is that we provide the ability to add multiple tags, youtube videos, and anyone can upload screenshots of the games they love

Users can also review games, like games, create playlists, and participate in discussions. It's like a mini community site.

Game developers are encouraged to add their games to the site provided they are playable. Games with logos, descriptions, and tags are also searchable with our built-in search engine.

Best of all, just log in with Reddit. Why Reddit? Because we love the /r/incremental_games community!
